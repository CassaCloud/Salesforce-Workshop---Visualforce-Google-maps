Module 1: Create Salesforce Developer Account

In this module you will create your own Salesforce account in your own cloud environment.

If you already have your development environmnet, you can skip this module.

  1. Open in a browser the following URL: https://developer.salesforce.com/join

  2. Fill in the signup form.

    • Enter real an email, you will need to open an activation email)
    • For Username, specify a unique user name in the form of an email address. for example: firsname.lastname@s1workshop.com (It doesn't have to be an existing email address: the Username is not used to send you emails)
    • When everything is populated, click the Sign Me Up button

  3. Check your email. You will receive an activation email for your Developer Edition Account.

  4. Click the link in the activation email. Enter your new password information, and click Save.

For login into your environmnet, use following URL: https://login.salesforce.com
